Getting Started with Mandrill

Reputation & Quota

How does the reputation system work?

Every Mandrill account has a reputation and hourly quota. These two features help protect us from spammers that would otherwise send a lot of emails quickly, that could then damage the reputation of our sending servers and negatively impact other Mandrill users.

How quickly will my hourly quota increase?

The hourly quota is designed to scale quickly if you need to send higher volumes. Each time you send a higher volume than you normally do, there's a period of adjustment as Mandrill learns about the new emails you're sending. If your account reputation remains good, the hourly quota shouldn't get in your way.

Can you manually increase my quota?

Account reputation and the hourly sending quota are handled automatically as you send email and Mandrill learns about the emails you're sending, and we cannot change either for you manually.
